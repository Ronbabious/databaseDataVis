Understanding the data categories

  • A specification for each data-item shown in the visualization. Multiple data-categories used throughout this visualization are created by Marty Kendall.
  • Nutritional information
    • Protein, Fat, Carbohydrates and Calories are listed as a value contained in a food item based on a 100 gram serving size.
  • ONIscore
    • Optimal nutrition intake is the percentage of essential macro and micro-nutrients fullfilled in a 2000 calorie serving
  • Insulin Index
    • Percentual increase of insulin during a two hour period after 250 calories are ingested.
  • Satiety
    • Based on a qualitative study on how filling 240 calories of a specific food item is perceived.
  • Nutrient Density
    • A measurement of how many nutrients contained in a single food item per calorie
